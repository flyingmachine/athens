Grateful Place

Hello, visitor,

Grateful Place is a wall-style site for people to consciously help lift each other up. If you'd like to belong to a community which supports you in leading a happier, more positive life, then this is the place for you :)

I was motivated to create this site after beginning a "daily gratitude" practice, where I'd think of something I was grateful for every morning. I started doing this after reading that it was an easy, powerful way to step off the "happiness treadmill", the constant striving for more things and accomplishments. Research shows that cultivating gratitude is a great way to become happier.

After a few months I noticed that I did in fact feel happier. Then I tried something new: sharing my gratitude on Facebook and Twitter. In doing so I learned that sharing my gratitude and seeing others' positive responses made me feel even happier.

So I made this site. Sharing gratitude is such a simple, easy way to become happier that I think anyone – including you – could benefit. Plus, it makes me happy to read about what other people are grateful for. Everyone wins!

If this sounds like the place for you, please join!

Sincerely,
Daniel Higginbotham
